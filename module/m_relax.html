rlx_run rlx_integrate

Performs structure relaxation (energy minimization) using steepest descent.

Atom positions evolve following the gradient direction, in steps of size tim_stp. If tim_stp is too large, bond constraints are likely to be violated.

If MPCD atoms are present, they do not take part in structure relaxation.
