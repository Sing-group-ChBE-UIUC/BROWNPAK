real(kind=rp), private, dimension(:), allocatable:: mass

(num_atoms_tot,) array. Stores mass of each atom (including MPCD atoms). The mass of each MPCD atom is taken as unity.
real(kind=rp), private, dimension(:), allocatable:: buf_aic

Buffer for atoms in cell storing mass, coordinates, & velocities. For nc atoms, the first nc elements stores the mass, the next 3*nc elements stores the coordinates, and the next 3*nc elements store the velocities. The velocities part of this buffer may be overwritten for relative velocity calculations.
